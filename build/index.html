What is Omniwave?

Omniwave is a solid and stable vertical phantom source that you can move around in.

Made from unobtrusive ОmniDrive 360 degrees radiators, a building stone for 3D audio without a ‘sweet spot’ that enables a free scalable listening area without limiting sight lines.

Enjoying natural sound through loudspeakers, you are all too aware that it’s reproduced. That’s because our ear is capable of identifying sound objects by their acoustic signature. Just small head movements will reveal that you’re connected exclusively to the loudspeaker’s acoustic signature, proving you are not listening the real thing.

A dreamed solution would be a loudspeaker that draws less attention to itself than to the sound it represents. It should be acoustically transparent, without adding or removing information while preserving sound stability all over the room. OmniWave, a revolutionary virtual speaker, is the first sound imaging device in the world to achieve this.
